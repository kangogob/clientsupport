How to Log in to the System

Follow these steps to log in to the system:

  1. - Click on the shortcut on the desktop. This should bring you to a login screen

  2. Ensure to type in the correct user ID and password(sensitive case). Press OK.

  3. This should bring you to the company select window.. From here, you can select the company to work on.

  4. Select the company and click OK

  5. Next choose the respective database and click OK to continue.
    This should load and bring you to the default Microsoft window.
